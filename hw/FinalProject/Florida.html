School To Watch

St. Thomas Aquinas

St. Thomas Aquinas is coming off of a 13-2 season that was lead by star defensive players like Anthony Soloman and Braylen Ingraham. St. Thomas consistently puts together championship level seasons, but you have to wonder who the new stars for the team will be. The defense will be lead by Penn St. commit Derek Wingo, and he will try to keep the championship level defense alive.

Senior To Watch

Carson Beck

The reigning Mr. Football in Florida helped turn Mandarin football from being a 2-8 team in 2017 to winning a state championship in 2018. The Georgia commit threw for over 3500 yards and 39 touchdowns in 14 games. After this season he will head to Georgia, and hopefully be the starter as soon as his true freshman season.
