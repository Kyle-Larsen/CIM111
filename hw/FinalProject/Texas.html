Team To Watch

Allen

The Allen Eagles gave their fans a good show in that college football level stadium last season. They finished the year 14-1, losing to Duncanville in the Texas State Championship. Allen lost its star offensive player Theo Wease last season, but like so many high school programs, next man up is key. Allen has been a consistent power in Texas, and there are no signs that there is going to be a drop.

Senior To Watch

RJ Mickens

Mickens is ranked as the top safety prospect in the nation by 247 Sports. He will be a four year starter for Southlake Carrol high school. He has established himself as threat in the back end, and a great ball hawk. He is a committed to the reigning National Champs, the Clemson Tigers.
