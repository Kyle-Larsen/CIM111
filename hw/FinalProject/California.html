School To Watch

Oaks Christian

Oaks Christian finished the season 12-1 losing to St. John Bosco of the semi-finals of the Division 1 State Championship. The Lions are losing two key players in, the former top player in the state, Kayvon Thibodeaux and QB and LB Josh Calvert. No doubt both of these losses will cause an impact, but the question that everyone is wondering is, "Is it enough to knock them out of division 1?" This is a team that was in division 1 for one season, and they are losing multiple star players. Do they have enough to keep up with the pack?

Senior To Watch

Kendall Milton

The silky smooth runner from Buchanan High School has led his team to winning seasons since his sophomore season. He is listed at 6'2 210lbs, but moves like a much smaller man. He ran for over 1300 yards last season with 27 touchdowns. His recruitment is still active, but as listed to be favoring the Ohlahoma Sooners.
