Info

Recruiting Corner is a website that likes to put together information about high school football in the states most well known for it. The states that are discussed in this website are Florida, California, Georgia, and Texas. These states have consistently produced the best high school teams, and the best high school players in the nation. Recruiting Corner goes over relevant information that could help you gain knowledge on some of the most elite players and teams in the nation.

About The Creator

Kyle Larsen has been working for the Recruiting and Player Personnel Department of the University of Miami football program for two years. During his time there he has gained knowledge in high school sports, and has been able to put that knowledge to use. Whether that be evaluating players or knowing where a recruit might commit, he has been on the inside of high school football, and wants to share this with other passionate football fans.
