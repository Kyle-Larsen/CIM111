School To Watch

Cartersville

The Carterville Hurricane's finished last season with a 14-1 record. They finished their season in the State Championship, losing to Blessed Trinity. Cartersville may be losing their leader in all-purpose yards in Marcus Gary, but they do retain their QB Tee Webb. Cartersville has been a powerhouse every since Trevor Lawrence's freshman season, and they don't seem to be on a downfall.

Senior To Watch

Tank Bigsby

The RB with the name that fits his style. Tank Bigsby rushed for almost 2250 yards last season for the Callaway Cavaliers. He is a power back that has plenty of speed, recently running a 4.50 forty yard dash. He'll be a leader for a Callaway team that is looking to build on the 11-3 season. Bigsby is not committed to any university at this time, but he is interested in South Carolina.
